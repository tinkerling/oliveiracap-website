About

A selective approach to meaningful innovation

Oliveira Capital holds a concentrated portfolio of investments in private technology and life sciences companies. We focus on businesses applying artificial intelligence to real-world challenges — including drug discovery, therapeutic development, and scientific reasoning.

We are long-term, patient investors. We maintain a selective approach and support the companies we back with a focus on durable value creation.

AI & Scientific Reasoning

Companies building systems that augment human intelligence and accelerate breakthroughs in research and analysis.

Life Sciences & Therapeutics

Companies applying computational approaches to disease biology, drug development, and precision medicine.

Frontier Technology

Foundational technology that enables new categories of products and discoveries.

Approach

We invest where artificial intelligence meets scientific and humanitarian need. Our focus is on companies whose work has the potential to materially improve how diseases are understood, how research is conducted, and how complex problems are solved.
